Rossett News Update – 7 June 2013

New look for Rossett

Why a new look …

· To project and reflect Rossett’s current culture

· To acknowledge academic achievement

· To celebrate 40 years of success (see below for more details)

So …

Now is the time to promote a visual identity emphasising Rossett’s outstanding teaching and learning

The benefits will be……

1. An identity that represents the inclusive and diverse nature of Rossett, acknowledging that our success is created and empowered by this

2. To reflect and represent the importance that we place on a rounded education of both curricular and extra curricular activity and the broad opportunities which exist at Rossett

3. To reflect the way students and staff feel about the school and our aspirations

We have already consulted with Staff, Governors, Students and a focus group of parents who were all very enthusiastic and excited about the new design as they feel it reflects the culture at Rossett. The design is still being worked on but I feel it is important to let the wider parent body see the way Rossett is moving forward. Please click here to see the first draft of the design.

Rossett 40 years on (1973-2013)

We are planning to celebrate our 40 year anniversary with an extravaganza on the last day of term. The exact details are still being developed but we know we want to include memories/reflections/memorabilia of Rossett throughout the decades.

Are there any parents/grandparents who have photos, memories that they would like to share to help the day be really special? If you have or would like to contribute please email mhowe@rossettschool.co.uk and we will get in touch to discuss.
