The 3Rs Culture of Learning

As a school we work together to help ensure that everyone develops the skills to enable them to enjoy and participate in their learning, deal with challenges that come their way and be successful. By focussing on the key skills of Responsibility, Resilience and Reflectiveness we anticipate our students will be able to make the most of their time at Rossett and leave us well equipped to take the next steps in life.
