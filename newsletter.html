Rossett News Update (local) – 11 October 2013

Coming in to school

Please be aware that, in line with our Safeguarding Policy, any parents coming in to Rossett must report to Reception before entering the school.

Year 7 Bewerley Park trip

Year 7s have the opportunity to attend an outdoor pursuit course 24-28 February 2014 which will include activities such as walking, ghyll scrambling, climbing, canoeing and orienteering. Places are offered on a ‘first come first served’ basis and the letter regarding this trip is available on the website under the Parents tab.

PTA Refreshment Coordinator

Can you help? The PTA urgently require someone to co-ordinate refreshments at parents’ evenings and other school events. The role will include ensuring there are sufficient supplies, updating the website with dates of events and emailing volunteers and showing them what to do at such events. If you would be willing to take on this role, either on your own or with a friend, the PTA would like to hear from you. For further information please email Helen Graham at info@rossettpta.co.uk

Sixth Form trip to St James' Radiotherapy Dept Open Day

Some of our Year 12 Biology and Applied Science students (with Mrs Robertson and Dr Strathdee) enjoyed an excellent visit to the Radiotherapy Department at St James’ last Saturday. Students were given a tour of the treatment facilities - please click the link to see photos: st-james-radiotherapy-department/

Sixth Form Rag Week w/b 21 October

An exciting week of lunchtime activities planned for each day – open to all students. Please send your child with some extra money to spend so they can join in. All money goes to charity.

One of the traditional and very popular events is ‘Rossett’s got talent’. The auditions for this are next week.

School Uniform Survey

We are gathering opinion on whether to change from our current school jumper to a blazer (to be phased in from September 2014 for our new Year 7 with other years to follow). I hope you will take time to complete this survey so that we can gauge opinion.

Author visit

On Wednesday we are delighted to welcome award winning Scottish author Stuart Reid who will be reading excerpts from his books to Year 7s fresh from his appearance at the Edinburgh Festival. We are expecting this to be an entertaining and inspiring event.

Congratulations to …

  • The Year 7 boys who beat St John Fisher 3-2 in the National Cup with the winning goal being scored in the last minute of the game!
  • The girls U13 football team who beat Allertonshire to reach the semi-final of the County Cup.
